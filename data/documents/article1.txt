Library Rules and Regulations

Welcome to our library! To ensure a pleasant experience for everyone, please adhere to the following rules:

1. Membership: Membership is free and open to all residents. Please register at the front desk with valid ID.
2. Borrowing: Members can borrow up to 5 items at a time for a period of 3 weeks. Renewals are possible if the item is not reserved.
3. Returns: Please return items by the due date to the return slot or front desk.
4. Fines: Overdue items incur a fine of $0.25 per day per item.
5. Conduct: Maintain a quiet environment, especially in designated study areas. No food or drinks allowed near computers or bookshelves. Mobile phones should be set to silent.
6. Computer Use: Library computers are available for research and educational purposes. Time limits may apply during busy periods.
7. Hours: Monday-Friday: 9 AM - 8 PM, Saturday: 10 AM - 5 PM, Sunday: Closed.
